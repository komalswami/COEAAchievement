About Our University

Mahatma Basaveshwar Education Society's College of Engineering Ambajogai was Established in 1993, Approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi & Affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere. It has a mission to develop a new generation of techno wizards who aspire to be the elite of the ever-growing Information Technology world. The college has built an ambiance where continuous learning, creativity and innovation are a way of life. The college supported by a strong group of well-qualified and competent faculty has a wide exposure of industry and academia.

Our Vision

*To facilitate transformation of students with technical knowledge to meet the changing needs of industry and society with human values..

Mision

* To offer state-of-the-art facilities for the students of all programs.
* To pursue academic excellence through student centric and dynamic Teaching-Learning process to encourage freedom of enquiry and openness to change among the students.
* To inculcate moral, ethical values and compassionate qualities in students overall development.

Values

*Committed to excellence We believe that performing to the highest standards on behalf of learners is fundamental to our identity as an organisation. *People working together We believe that our potential can only be achieved in a collaboration environment of common purpose and shared success.*Progressive spirit We believe that challenging the status quo in changing world demands innovative thinking and a dedication to growth.
